Welcome to My Renewable Energy Portfolio

This portfolio showcases three of my key projects in renewable energy that I'm passionate about, including solar photovoltaic systems, solar water heating, and biomass biogas solutions. Each project highlights a sustainable energy system designed to reduce environmental impact and promote clean technology adoption. Navigate using the sidebar to explore each project in detail.

Solar Photovoltaic (PV) System

Source of Energy

Sunlight is the primary energy source captured by photovoltaic cells in solar panels.

Conversion Process

Solar panels convert sunlight directly into electricity using the photovoltaic effect.

Output/Utilization

Electricity generated powers homes, industries, and can be fed into the electrical grid.

Real-world Relevance

Widely adopted for sustainable power generation, reducing dependence on fossil fuels and emissions.

Solar Water Heating System

Source of Energy

Solar thermal energy captured by solar collector panels.

Conversion Process

Solar collectors absorb sunlight and heat water circulated via pipes through the system.

Output/Utilization

Heated water is used for domestic purposes like bathing, cleaning, and space heating.

Real-world Relevance

Reduces energy bills and reliance on conventional water heating methods using fossil fuels.

Biomass Biogas System

Source of Energy

Organic biomass such as agricultural waste, manure, and food residues.

Conversion Process

Anaerobic digestion of biomass produces biogas mainly composed of methane and carbon dioxide.

Output/Utilization

Biogas can be used for cooking, heating, or electricity generation; leftover slurry for fertilizer.

Real-world Relevance

Offer sustainable waste management and renewable energy, especially in rural areas.
